How it works

  1. Upload a CSV of students with their photos.
  2. Start a live session pointing at the classroom CCTV (RTSP) or webcam.
  3. System captures frames continuously, recognizing faces every 2 seconds.
  4. Every 60 minutes, an hourly report is auto-generated.
  5. At end of day, students appearing in ≥75% of hourly reports are marked Present.
  6. Download individual hourly reports + final daily attendance as CSV.
